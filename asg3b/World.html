Add More Blocks to Activiate the Animal

You first look at the top of the tower, press S to zoom out to view the world

Animal will appear if 5 blocks or more are created

Press "W", "S", "A", "D", "Q", or "E" to get on same level as blocks.

Camera Angle

Key commands:
W - move the camera forward;
A - move the camera to the left;
S - move the camera backwards;
D - move the camera to the right;
Q - rotate left
E - rotate right
N - add new block
Backsapce key - delete the block that last added


Animation: To stop the poke please press ON button then press OFF button.
